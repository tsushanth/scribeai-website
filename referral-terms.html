Creator Program Terms

Last updated: December 28, 2025

1. Program Overview

The Scribe AI Creator Program ("Program") allows approved content creators ("Creators") to earn revenue by promoting Scribe AI to their audience. By participating in the Program, you agree to these terms.

2. Eligibility

To participate in the Program, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have an active social media presence or content platform
  • Submit an application and be approved by Scribe AI
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Have a PayPal account or other approved payment method for receiving payments

3. How the Program Works

Referral Links

Upon approval, you will receive a unique referral link. When users download and subscribe to Scribe AI using your link, you earn a commission.

Attribution

Referrals are tracked using cookies that last 30 days. If a user clicks your link and subscribes within 30 days, the referral is attributed to you.

Qualifying Actions

You earn commission when a referred user:

  • Downloads the Scribe AI app
  • Creates an account
  • Converts to a paid subscription (after any trial period)

4. Commission Structure

Commission rates and structure will be communicated to you upon program acceptance. Rates may vary based on:

  • Your audience size and engagement
  • Content quality and relevance
  • Historical performance

Scribe AI reserves the right to modify commission rates with 30 days notice.

5. Payment Terms

  • Minimum Payout: $50 USD minimum before payment is processed
  • Payment Schedule: Payments are processed monthly, typically within 30 days of month end
  • Payment Methods: PayPal or bank transfer (where available)
  • Currency: All payments are in USD
  • Taxes: You are responsible for any taxes on your earnings

6. Promotional Guidelines

When promoting Scribe AI, you must:

  • Clearly disclose your affiliate relationship (e.g., "This is a sponsored link" or "#ad")
  • Provide honest and accurate information about the app
  • Not make false or misleading claims about features or benefits
  • Follow FTC guidelines and local advertising regulations
  • Not use spam, unsolicited messages, or deceptive tactics

Prohibited Activities

You may NOT:

  • Use paid advertising that bids on Scribe AI brand terms
  • Create fake reviews or testimonials
  • Make income claims or guarantees
  • Use your own referral link for personal subscriptions
  • Generate fake clicks, downloads, or subscriptions
  • Promote Scribe AI on adult, violent, or illegal content

7. Content Guidelines

Your promotional content should:

  • Be original and authentic to your brand
  • Demonstrate genuine use or understanding of the app
  • Target audiences interested in education, productivity, or learning
  • Comply with platform terms of service (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.)

8. Intellectual Property

You may use Scribe AI logos, screenshots, and promotional materials provided by us. You may not:

  • Modify our logos or branding
  • Register domain names containing "Scribe AI" or similar terms
  • Create apps or services that impersonate Scribe AI

9. Termination

Either party may terminate participation in the Program at any time. Scribe AI may immediately terminate your participation for:

  • Violation of these terms
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Damage to Scribe AI's reputation
  • Inactivity for 6 or more months

Upon termination, unpaid commissions above the minimum threshold will be paid within 60 days.

10. Program Changes

Scribe AI reserves the right to modify or discontinue the Program at any time. We will provide 30 days notice for material changes to commission rates or program structure.

11. Limitation of Liability

Scribe AI is not responsible for lost commissions due to technical issues, user cancellations, or refunds. Our maximum liability is limited to unpaid commissions owed to you.

12. Relationship

Participation in the Program does not create an employment, partnership, or agency relationship. You are an independent contractor responsible for your own taxes and business expenses.

13. Confidentiality

Commission rates, program details, and any non-public information shared with you must be kept confidential and not disclosed to third parties.

14. Contact

For questions about the Creator Program, contact us at:

  • Email: creators@scribeai.app
